we are committed to

Creating an impact

For our customers, society, employees
as well as the planet

We strive to create Social, Environmental and Economic (SEE) value by making the best use of products in the reverse supply chain, avoiding wastage and landfills

SOCIAL

Reverse inventory is made available for startups, health clinics, schools and other organizations.

ENVIRONMENTAL

Reuse of products in the reverse supply chain results in reduced wastage and hence, a cleaner, greener planet.

ECONOMIC

By providing smaller retailers direct access to reverse inventory, we foster entrepreneurship.

How we are bringing the change

2M+

Refurbished laptops deployed

With the help of multiple enterprises and NASSCOM foundation, we have provided refurbished laptops to schools and health facilities.

0.5M+

Enabling start-up ecosystem by making technology affordable for start-ups, including PCs, laptops and other IT equipment.
